Introduction

The New Age Pro Era is a protessional sports management company that serves athletes by providing sports
marketing and talent management. New Age Pro Era was founded in January 2022 and legally registered in August 2022
bearing Reg: 2022/670972/07. Since the foundation, New Age Pro Era has been able to help a lot of athletes.

Vision

To completely transform the sports agency industry such that agents not only provide exeptional representation
for the athletes they represent but also have a positive impact on them.

Mission

To improve the caliber and worth of sports in South Africa by offering top-notch services that give our partners and
employees priceless experience.
